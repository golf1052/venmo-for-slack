What is YHackSlackPack?

A collection of slack integrations to make your life easier and your work flow-ier. Interactions with the external services are text-based, via a bot. There are 3 integrations provided out-of-the-box, but they're all built on a framework that makes it easy to add more. All you need is an API!

Venmo

Venmo...through Slack.
It works.
You have no excuse for not paying your boss for lunch.

Tinder

Swipe right without taking out your phone.
Uses Facebook for authentication.

Uber

It's like Uber for Uber, through Slack.
This feature is still under development.
